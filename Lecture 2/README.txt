Topic 2 - Lecture Presentation
==============================

HOW TO OPEN:
  Double-click "index.html". It opens in any web browser
  (Chrome, Edge, Firefox). No internet connection required.

WHAT TO COPY:
  Keep "index.html" and "favicon.svg" together in the same folder.
  (favicon.svg is just the browser-tab icon; the slides still work
   without it, but keep it for completeness.)

NOTES:
  - Everything is self-contained in index.html (about 19 MB).
  - Works fully offline.
